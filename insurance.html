Insurance FAQ

Quick answers about cover, claims and costs.

Can I insure my shamba (farm)? ➕
Yes — we cover maize, beans, livestock, and even poultry against drought, floods, and disease. Talk to our agent for a simple assessment.
How do I file a claim? ➕
Call our toll-free line 0800 123 456 or simply send "CLAIM" to 12345. We'll call you back within 2 hours.
Is there an age limit? ➕
For life cover, we accept members from 18 to 70 years. Health and accident plans cover all ages — children included.
Can I pay daily / weekly? ➕
Absolutely. Most plans offer daily, weekly or monthly M-Pesa payments. No penalty if you miss — we'll send a reminder.
Do you cover pre-existing conditions? ➕
Some of our health plans cover chronic conditions after a 3-month waiting period. Chat with our agent to find the right fit.
