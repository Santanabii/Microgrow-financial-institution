Loan FAQ

Answers to common questions.

What do I need to qualify? ➕
Kenyan ID, active M-Pesa line, and a consistent income (business or employment). No collateral needed.
How fast will I receive the money? ➕
If approved before 4pm, money is sent to your M-Pesa within 1 hour. Most approvals are instant.
Can I repay early? ➕
Yes — no penalty. You only pay interest for the days you used the money.
What if I miss a payment? ➕
We’ll send a reminder. A one‑time late fee of 2% applies, not daily compounding. We work with you to restructure if needed.
Do you offer loans to groups? ➕
Yes — we specialise in group loans (chamas, village banks). Higher limits and shared responsibility.
