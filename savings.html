Savings FAQ

Everything you need to know about saving with us.

Is my money safe? ➕
Absolutely. We are licensed by the Central Bank of Kenya and your savings are protected by the Deposit Insurance Fund up to KSh 250,000 per account.
How do I withdraw money? ➕
Via M-Pesa, agent, or branch. Withdrawals from your phone are instant, 24/7. First withdrawal each month is free.
What is the minimum balance? ➕
There's no minimum balance for Everyday Saver and GoalSave. For LockSave, the minimum is KSh 5,000.
How is interest calculated? ➕
Interest is calculated on your daily balance and paid quarterly. The more you save, the more you earn.
Can I open an account for my child? ➕
Yes — FutureSave is designed for under-18s. You'll need the child's birth certificate and your ID. The account is jointly managed.
