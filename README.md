# Microgrow-financial-institution
This project contains the official company pages built with HTML and Tailwind CSS.  
It's designed to be clean, modern, and easy to navigate — reflecting MicroGrow's mission of transparent, accessible finance for Kenyans.


**PAGE BREAKDOWN**
• Landing page-website name. a background image that is relevant, short description of the specific microfinance institution
. About microfinance and the institution- Definition of microfinance, Brief history of the institution, Purpose and importance and Who it serves (low-income earners, small businesses, rural communities).
• Services offered- micro-loans, savings accounts, financial literacy, training, insurance services and group lending programs.
• How it works- the application process, loan assessments, repayment plan, monitoring and support.
• Contact page- How to reach the website's main HQ



**Key features**
- Clean, trustworthy blue/cyan and whitegit financial theme
- Fully responsive (mobile-first design)
- Hero section with strong call-to-action
- Services showcase (loans, savings, insurance)
- Impact statistics & trust signals (CBK licensed, repayment rate, etc.)
- About page with mission/vision, leadership, timeline, partners, FAQ
- Simple, conversion-focused layout for loan applications

**Background story**
MicroGrow started with a simple belief: fair finance should be for everyone — no collateral, no hoops.
From a single community hall in Nairobi to 18,000+ clients across urban and rural Kenya, we’ve stayed true to that promise. We prioritize women entrepreneurs, smallholder families, and the resilience of the informal sector.
Today we’re CBK-licensed and proud to be one of the most trusted microfinance partners in the country.
