Our Story

MicroGrow started with a simple belief: fair finance should be for everyone — no collateral, no hoops.

From a single community hall in Nairobi to 18,000+ clients across urban and rural Kenya, we’ve stayed true to that promise. We prioritize women entrepreneurs, smallholder families, and the resilience of the informal sector.

Today we’re CBK-licensed and proud to be one of the most trusted microfinance partners in the country.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MicroGrow? ➕

No. We rely on group guarantees, cash flow, and character — zero traditional collateral.
